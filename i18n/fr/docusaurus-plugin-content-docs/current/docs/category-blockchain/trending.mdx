# New, Trending, and Top Content

Categories, Channel pages, and Tag searches are sorted by New, Trending, and Top.

**New** is simply content in the order it was uploaded.

**Trending** shows the content that has had the greatest increase in tips and supports over the last few hours and days.

**Top** shows the content published within the selected period that has received the largest total amount of tips and supports
