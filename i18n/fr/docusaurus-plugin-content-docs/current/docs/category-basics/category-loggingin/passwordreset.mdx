# Resetting Your Password

If you don't know your password, your first option is to hit **Forgot Password?**

From here, you'll re-enter your email, click **Reset Password**, and we'll send you an email to reset your password.

![](https://i.imgur.com/gEXRleY.png)

Follow the link on the email to set a new password.

![](https://i.imgur.com/aoSVH7I.png)
