# Password Changes

While logged into your account, you can change your password in the **Settings** menu.

Click your channel icon in the top right corner, and select **Settings** in the dropdown menu.

![](https://i.imgur.com/jnjajPu.jpg)

From here, you'll scroll down to **Account** and click **Manage** on the **Password** tab.

Then, you'll simply enter your old password, what you want your new password to be, and hit **Set Password**.

![](https://i.imgur.com/OKtlKuY.png)
