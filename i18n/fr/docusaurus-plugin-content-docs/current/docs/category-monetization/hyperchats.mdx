# Hyperchats & Comment Tips

## Hyperchats

Hyperchats are highlighted chat during livestreams. These are sent by attaching a tip to the chat.

After typing your chat, you can send a hyperchat by selecting either the LBC icon or dollar sign under the chat box.

From here, you'll select an amount and hit **Send.**

Your hyperchat will be highlighted and show the amount tipped.

## Comment Tips

Comment Tips are sent by attaching a tip to a comment.

After typing your comment, you can send a hyperchat by selecting either the LBC icon or dollar sign under the comment box.

From here, you'll select an amount and hit **Send.**

Your comment will show the amount tipped.
