# Sending Cash Tips

## Adding a Payment Method

Before sending a cash tip, you'll need to have a payment method added. 

To add a card, you'll first need to open your wallet. Your wallet is towards the top right of the screen, on the menu bar.

![](/img/en/category-basics17.png)

On the mobile version, you'll find it towards the bottom of the sidebar menu.

![](/img/en/category-basics18.png)


From here, you'll select **Payment Methods**.

![](/img/en/category-basics23.png)

On the Payment Methods screen, you'll simply enter your full name, card number, its expiration date, and its 3 digit CVC code.

![](/img/en/category-basics24.png)

If you already have a card added, it will appear on the Payment Methods screen.

## Sending Cash Tips

To send a tip, you'll click the **Support** button under an upload.

![](/img/en/category-monetization6.png)

This option is also available on channels.

![](/img/en/category-monetization7.png)

From this menu, you'll select the **Tip** button with the USD symbol, select an amount, and click **Send Tip**.

:::info Information

For your bank account connection to be successful on Odysee, you will need to have a dollar bank account. Some regions or countries are not yet eligible for this, so you will need to check availability in your country when connecting your bank account or card.

:::
