# Portal Location

You can access the Creator Portal within the Memberships landing page. Click the avatar icon at the top right corner and click on My Memberships.

![memberships](https://thumbs.odycdn.com/f458f5cfc1a5e17b24f7a5370337b018.webp)

After navigating to the Memberships landing page, you'll see two portals.

![memberships](https://thumbs.odycdn.com/c8e5e4ef94d294330f0e9a18784fc661.webp)

To manage your own tiers as a creator, click the **Manage Memberships** button under Membership Portal.
