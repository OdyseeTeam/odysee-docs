# Menu Location

To access the creator settings menu, click the avatar icon at the top right corner (if you already have a channel, this icon will be your channel's profile picture), and click on **Channels**.

![](/img/en/category-channels1.png)

On mobile, the settings menu will be towards the bottom of the extended sidebar.

![](/img/en/category-channels2.png)

From the channels page, select a channel. Your creator settings will be the same for all of your channels.

![](/img/en/category-channels11.png)

From your channel page, navigate to the **Settings** tab.

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/80921344/201973918-d2d17918-9562-468b-9ecf-73189d734612.png)
