# Receiving AR Tips

By receiving a tip on AR, you're receiving community support for your channel's content. This tip will be transferred directly to your Odysee wallet. You can view all tips received on your wallet page.

![wallet](https://thumbs.odycdn.com/e6e0da12567980d225af59fdfb317f86.webp)

From this screen, you'll select **Balance** and switch to the **Tip** tab. There you'll see all the tips you've received.

![Tips](https://thumbs.odycdn.com/04649ce7117cffdf0fcc6a6859401177.webp)
