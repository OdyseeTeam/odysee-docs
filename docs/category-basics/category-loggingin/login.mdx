# Logging Into Odysee

Do you already have an Odysee account and are returning to the application? You'll click the **Log In** button on the top right corner.

![](/img/en/category-basics7.png)

On mobile, you'll hit the person icon, which will show Log In in the dropdown menu.

![](/img/en/category-basics8.jpg)

You'll start by entering your email.

![](/img/en/category-basics9.png)

On the password screen, you have a few options.

![](/img/en/category-basics10.png)

If you know your password and want to log in, you'll simply enter your password and hit Continue. Alternatively, you can also have a [Magic link](https://help.odysee.tv/category-basics/category-loggingin/loginlinks/) sent to your email address or [reset your password](https://help.odysee.tv/category-basics/category-loggingin/passwordreset).
